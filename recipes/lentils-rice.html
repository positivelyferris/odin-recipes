Lentils and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Amy's Lentil soup
  • 2 cups rice of choice (recommend calrose white rice)
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, pour Amy's Lentil soup and turn heat to low.

  2. Using a rice cooker, place 2 cups of rice and set to cook.

  3. Once rice finishes, heat up a skillet to medium heat and fry eggs (use avocado oil to make healthier).

  4. Plate the rice, then add lentil soup on top with fried eggs as the last layer.

  5. Serve and enjoy this simple yet delicious meal!
