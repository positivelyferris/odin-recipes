Blackstone Smash Burgers

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef (recommend using Wagyu beef for extra flavor!)
  • 6 Hamburger buns
  • Seasoned salt to taste
  • 12 American cheese slices
  • Avocado oil for grill surface
  • Additional hamburger toppings as desired

Steps

  1. Using a 1/3 cup measuring spoon, loosely pack ground beef and place meat balls on wax paper.

  2. Set out hamburger buns to have ready to toast on Blackstone grill.

  3. Preheat Blackstone grill to medium-high heat (around 425 degrees fahrenheit)as you want the meat to sear. Pick another section of grill and set to low for the hamburger buns.

  4. Using avocado oil, drizzle a small amount onto heated grill surface and spread with spatula.

  5. Place hamburger buns (cut side facing down) onto low heat section of grill.

  6. Place first meat ball down onto oiled grill surface, then press firmly for around 10 seconds with a cast-iron grill press.

  7. Lift grill press, then flip burger patty onto other side.

  8. Season patty with seasoned salt to taste.

  9. Cook another 15-20 seconds, then place American cheese slice on patty to melt.

  10. Plate toasted buns, then using spatula, place cooked patty with cheese onto buns.

  11. Repeat with remaining meat balls and buns you have made. Top with your favorite hamburger toppings and enjoy!
